Why did you decide to become a web developer?

My career has revolved around me having ideas and then explaining them to a team of developers to create them. By becoming a web developer, I will be able to bring those ideas to life myself and to learn about some of the limitations and try to come up with new ways to overcome them.

What is your design process?

Design starts with a mockup of your page including details about navigation items and the color palette. Once approved, we will work together to build content for each section and to gather the assets that you want to share with your visitors. After final approval, the site will be built with daily updates showing progress and giving you a chance to give feedback.

Who are your clients?

My services are geared towards local businesses in the Jacksonville, FL area.

What is your average project turnaround?

Once the specifications are finalized, the project is usually done within two weeks!
